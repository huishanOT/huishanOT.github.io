Hi! I’m Sabrina
#

I’m Sabrina, a new grad occupational therapist with a passion for education. I was born and raised in Kaoshiung, Taiwan and completed my occupational therapy degree at National Taiwan University. I’m fully licensed in Taiwan and pursuing my master’s degree in the US.

Whether through public speaking or courses, sharing my own experience and knowledge to support and encourage others is very fulfilling. Whenever people tell me that my resources and support have helped them, it gives my work true meaning.

About this blog
#

In 2022, i started to document my study method on Instagram (@33late_)    Surprisingly, these experiences resonated with people and my community began to grow, which was truly unexpected for me.

I created this blog because I wanted to document my journey and simplify my professional knowledge to support parents and children. I received so much help when I was a student, and writing these articles is my way of giving back and continuing that circle of kindness.
